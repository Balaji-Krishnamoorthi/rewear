How it started 👟

It started with a cupboard full of clothes that no longer fit — but were too good to throw away. We thought, why not help others in Coimbatore find these gems at affordable prices?


ReWear is built for students, working professionals, and anyone who loves fashion but hates overpaying. We connect sellers with buyers — simple, fast, and free.
